## Ingredients

### For the Curry:
- 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 large onion, finely diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2-inch piece fresh ginger, grated
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
- 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- Salt and black pepper to taste

### For Serving:
- Cooked basmati rice
- Fresh cilantro, chopped
- Lime wedges
- Naan bread (optional)

## Instructions

### Step 1: Prepare the Chicken
1. Cut the chicken thighs into 1-inch pieces and season with salt and pepper
2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat
3. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes
4. Remove chicken from pot and set aside

### Step 2: Build the Sauce
1. In the same pot, add diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes
2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant
3. Add all the spices (curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, cayenne) and cook for 30 seconds to toast them

### Step 3: Simmer the Curry
1. Return the chicken to the pot
2. Add coconut milk, chicken broth, and tomato paste
3. Stir well to combine all ingredients
4. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low
5. Cover and simmer for 30-35 minutes, stirring occasionally
6. The curry is ready when the chicken is tender and the sauce has thickened

### Step 4: Serve
1. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper
2. Serve hot over basmati rice
3. Garnish with fresh cilantro and lime wedges
4. Serve with naan bread on the side

## Tips & Variations

- **Make it Vegetarian**: Replace chicken with chickpeas or tofu
- **Adjust Spice Level**: Increase or decrease cayenne pepper to your preference
- **Add Vegetables**: Stir in spinach, peas, or bell peppers in the last 5 minutes of cooking
- **Make Ahead**: This curry tastes even better the next day! Store in the refrigerator for up to 3 days
