## Ingredients

- 2 cups Japanese short-grain rice
- 2 cups water
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1.5 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt

## Instructions

1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
2. Combine rice and water in a rice cooker and cook according to the manufacturerâ€™s instructions.
3. In a small saucepan, combine vinegar, sugar, and salt. Heat until dissolved, then cool.
4. When rice is cooked, transfer to a large bowl. Gently fold in the vinegar mixture with a rice paddle.
5. Let the rice cool to room temperature before using for sushi.

## Tips

- Use a wooden bowl (hangiri) for best results.
- Fan the rice as you mix to give it a glossy finish.
